# Code Of Conduct

Delegation Bot should be a serious, welcoming project for people building
trustworthy AI systems.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and specific.
- Assume good intent, but accept correction.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Make space for new contributors.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Demeaning language
- Sharing private information without permission
- Spam, scams, or manipulative promotion
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block contributors who
make the project unsafe or unproductive.

If there is a problem, contact the maintainer through GitHub. A dedicated
security/contact email should be added before a broad public launch.
